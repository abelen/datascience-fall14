Detailed item info

Product Information
Akira, Shun-di, Kage, and Pai are back -- but this time, they're kids! Virtua Fighter Kids brings home the arcade action to the Sega Saturn. This port of the arcade game is based on the award-winning coin-op, Virtua Fighter. Featuring ten childlike fighters, each of whom sports a huge array of moves. With oversized heads and undersized arms, Virtua Fighter Kids puts a new twist on a proven genre.

Fighting takes place in three dimensional rings, allowing players full freedom of movement. Most of the modes of play are the same as Virtua Fighter 2, such as Ranking, Arcade, and Practice. Adding to the legacy of the Virtua Fighter series, Virtua Fighter Kids includes a new play mode and a new feature: Kids mode and Combo Maker. Kids mode simplifies the controls by allowing combos to be performed with one button. The Combo Maker gives players the ability to create up to 20 new moves. Take the battle into your own hands!
