    Detailed item info

    Product Information
    Sonic Jam is a compilation of Sega's famous hedgehog's hits. The CD includes the full versions of Sonic the Hedgehog, Sonic the Hedgehog 2, Sonic the Hedgehog 3, and Sonic & Knuckles -- along with a 3D museum full of artwork, videos, and other Sega memorabilia.

    The original titles' platform gameplay remains intact, with plenty of running, jumping, and Super Sonic Spin-dashing against the evil Doctor Robotnik. Doctor Robotnik is the mad scientist who enjoys unleashing his mechanical creations on Sonic's Disney-esque island paradise.

    New difficulty levels have been added; younger players can adopt an easy mode, while more experienced Sonic fans can go up against ultra-difficult renditions of the Sonic classics. Get ready for an attitude-packed trip down memory lane with Sonic Jam.
