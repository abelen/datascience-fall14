    Detailed item info

    Product Information
    Albert Odyssey: Legend of Eldean was originally released for the Super Nintendo system in Japan. It contains a menu-driven combat system that is turn-based. You control Pike and the other four members of his party. Pike is a young orphan who was raised by harpies. During the first ten years of his life all he has ever known is the peaceful ways of the forest. Now, seemingly from nowhere, monsters have begun attacking humans and violence has erupted in this magical land. When Pike's village is attacked he and his adopted sister, Laia, are turned to stone. Cirrus, a magical sword once held by his father, is able to rescue Pike but not Laia. Pike must embark on a quest in order to find a way to free his sister from stone.
